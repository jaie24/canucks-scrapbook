My Journey as a Canucks Fan

First Ever Canucks Game vs Calgary Flames

On the 12th of November, I went to my first ever Canucks game! I’ve always been intrigued by hockey as a sport ever since moving to Calgary. At the time, though, I didn’t have many people around me who could teach me about the game or share that interest. But since moving to Vancouver, a good friend of mine who’s also into hockey suggested we finally go to a game together — and I’m so glad we did. The energy in the arena was incredible, and it gave me a whole new appreciation for the sport. P.S the Flames jersey was a hand-me-down from my cousin so I wore it to my first game! Still a loyal Canucks fan :D

Game vs San Jose Sharks

For my third Canucks game, I went to the Christmas Eve game where they played against the San Jose Sharks. It was a wonderful festive atmosphere, and what made it even more special was that I got to share the experience with a new friend. I had recently met a French exchange student, and after chatting about hockey, I convinced her to come to the game with me. It was her first time at a hockey game too, so it felt really meaningful to experience it together especially since we were both away from our families during Christmas.

PWHL Vancouver

Went to the PWHL Toronto vs Montreal game in Rogers Arena to support women in sports! Multitasking watching the Canucks away game during the beginning though!

Member Open Skate Event

Went to a member open skate event where a friend got me two tickets for! Got to meet Finn!

Last Canucks Game vs Vegas Knights

Last Canucks game of the season vs Vegas Knights. It was an emotional night since they lost that night and could also be Boeser's last game.
